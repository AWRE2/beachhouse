The Community

Resort living,
redefined

Dominion Beach is one of Estepona's most exclusive beachfront communities — a private, gated paradise with every amenity, steps from the sea.

Direct Beach Access

Step from the community garden directly onto a beautiful, quiet stretch of Mediterranean beach. Sun loungers and shade await just steps away.

Two Swimming Pools

Two large, beautifully maintained pools set within lush tropical gardens — perfect for morning laps or long, lazy afternoons in the Andalucían sun.

Beachfront Restaurant

An exclusive residents-only restaurant right on the water's edge. Fresh Mediterranean cuisine, sea views, and impeccable service.

Fitness Centre

A fully equipped gym within the resort. Start your morning with a workout, then head to the beach — the ultimate healthy holiday routine.

Coastal Promenade

A scenic path running directly alongside the property — perfect for morning runs or relaxed evening strolls as the sun sets over the sea.

24/7 Gated Security

Peace of mind comes as standard within this fully secured community. Relax completely, knowing you are in one of Estepona's most exclusive enclaves.
